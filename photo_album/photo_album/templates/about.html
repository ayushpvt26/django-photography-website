About

I'm Daniel, an amateur photographer from the UK. I've created this site to house all my photography from the various events I go.

I started taking photographs at the Eurogamer Expo in 2011 and have been working hard to improve as a photographer ever since. There's a long way to go, but overtime I've improved ten fold and aim to continue learning all I can.

I love taking photos of people and would love the opportunity to do a photoshoot with someone, so if you're interested in a time for prints deal let me know!
